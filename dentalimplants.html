Dental Implants: Your Permanent Solution

Dental implants provide a strong, permanent foundation for replacement teeth that look, feel, and function just like natural teeth. This modern technology restores the comfort and confidence to eat, speak, and smile fully.

Key Benefits

  • Bone Preservation: Tiny titanium posts prevent jawbone deterioration.
  • Full Function: Regain the ability to eat virtually anything comfortably.
  • Natural Appearance: Seamlessly blend with your existing smile.

Initial Evaluation & Planning

The process begins with a comprehensive dental/radiographic examination and health history review. During this consultation, Dr. Ghia or a referred Oral and Maxillofacial Surgeon will create a personalized treatment plan for your specific needs, which may include bone grafting.

Advanced Care & Team Effort

We utilize recent advances like single-stage implants (requiring fewer surgeries). The implant placement is a team effort: Dr. Ghia performs the surgery, and your restorative dentist designs and fits the final permanent crown or bridge.

Minimizing surgical procedures where possible is a priority.

The Standard Timeline: 6–8 Months to a New Smile

Phase One: Placement & Osseointegration (3–6 Months)

The metal anchors are surgically placed into the jawbone. Over the next three to six months, the implants are protected beneath the gums, bonding directly with the jawbone—a critical process called osseointegration .

You wear temporary dentures and maintain a soft diet while your restorative dentist plans the final aesthetic design.

Phase Two: Restoration & Final Crown Placement

After successful bonding, the surgeon uncovers the implant and attaches a small healing collar . Dr. Ghia takes an impression to fabricate the custom posts/attachments and secure the permanent replacement teeth (crown or bridge).

Most patients experience minimal disruption and achieve their final restoration in 6-8 months.
