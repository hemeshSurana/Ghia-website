Meet The Staff

MEET THE STAFF

At Callfield Dental, we believe exceptional care starts with an exceptional team. Our dedicated professionals combine experience, compassion, and a genuine commitment to helping every patient feel comfortable and confident in their smile. Each team member plays an important role in creating a welcoming environment and delivering the high-quality care our Wichita Falls community deserves. Get to know the talented individuals who make Callfield Dental a trusted name in hometown dentistry.

Christy - Dental Hygienist

I have been with our team since 2011. I attended Midwestern State University, where I earned my Bachelor of Science degree in dental hygiene and graduated with honors. I love working with our patients and enjoy helping them keep their teeth healthy and clean, as well as providing education about proper at-home dental care. My husband, Nick, and I have three boys who keep us busy and bring us so much joy. In my free time, I enjoy crafting and sewing. I am also a member of Faith Baptist Church in Iowa Park.

Jessica - Dental Hygienist

I was born and raised in Vernon, Texas, and graduated from Northside ISD before attending Amarillo College. There, I earned my associate degree in dental hygiene with highest honors and was honored with the Outstanding Major Award. Since becoming a licensed hygienist in 2018, I have gained extensive experience and hold certification in diode laser therapy. I was thrilled to join this practice in February 2025. I’m married to my high school sweetheart, Ty, and together we have two wonderful daughters, Macy and Gracy. Outside of the office, I enjoy cooking, weightlifting, cheering on my girls at their soccer games, and spending quality time with my family. My favorite part of being a dental hygienist is helping patients feel comfortable and cared for while providing the highest level of treatment in a welcoming environment.

Kayla - Lead dental assistant

Hi, my name is Kayla! I’ve been a dental assistant for over three years, with most of my experience in oral surgery. Now working in general dentistry, I’ve truly found a place where I belong. My favorite part of what I do is connecting with patients—I look forward to seeing each of you every day. I strive to create a caring and lighthearted environment, because I know visiting the dentist can feel stressful for many. Outside of work, I enjoy spending time with my wonderful family, my two playful puppies, and close friends. I’m also building my small business, Haven in the Pines, and continuing to grow in my faith with God. Traveling is another passion of mine, especially when it takes me through beautiful mountain and forest scenery.

J’la - Dental assistant

My name is J’La Sanchez. I have been chairside assisting for 2+ years. I love being able to help and bond with patients alongside our wonderful team. I have two little boys at home with my fiancé. We enjoy spending time together, being outdoors, going on nature walks, doing arts, crafts, and trying new things. I look forward to seeing you all :)!

Gracie - Dental assistant

My name is Gracie Looney. I received my dental assistant license in 2024 and have been assisting Dr. Ghia since 2025. I love building meaningful connections with our patients and enjoy working alongside such a supportive team. In my free time, I volunteer at First Baptist Church, where I work with children, coaching cheer and support our college and student ministry alongside my fiancé Michael

Kimberly – Insurance & Financial Coordinator

Kimberly joined our team in 2020. She helps our patients understand their dental insurance benefits and works with them to help them apply those benefits to their dental care. She can help you with general questions, dental insurance, or payment options!

Nora - Front desk receptionist

My name is Nora, and I have dedicated over 31 years to working as a dental front desk receptionist. Throughout my career, I have enjoyed being the welcoming face patients see when they arrive and ensuring their experience is smooth and comfortable. Outside of work, I cherish my identity as a twin, which has been a special part of who I am. In my free time, I love staying active by working out, connecting with friends and family, and exploring new places through travel.
