Upper Jaw Implant Advantage

While the treatment options are similar to the lower jaw, the softer upper jaw bone often requires more implants for support. The critical benefit is the possibility of eliminating the need to cover the roof of your mouth (the palate) with a complete denture.

This allows you to fully taste your food and better sense its temperature, making your denture feel significantly more natural.
