Professional At-Home Teeth Whitening

Achieve the bright, beautiful smile you've been dreaming of with our safe, quick, and inexpensive custom whitening procedure. We offer a simple, proven method for creating a brilliant, confident smile.

The Custom Whitening Process

1. Custom Splints

We take a simple impression to create your specialized, custom-fit clear trays (splints/stents).

2. Bleaching Agent

You receive a special, professional-grade bleaching agent to place inside the custom splints.

3. Daily Wear

Wear the splints for a few hours per day (or overnight) for about one to two weeks until you reach the desired brightness.

Key Whitening Benefits

  • Corrects discoloration from staining, aging, or chemical damage.
  • Works for people of any age.
  • Safe—does not alter tooth structure or existing dental work.

Important Considerations

For extreme tooth discoloration, crowns or veneers may be the only permanent solution. However, due to the low cost and effectiveness, professional whitening is almost always recommended as the first option.

*Note: Over-the-counter products are available, and we are happy to discuss their effectiveness relative to our professional-grade solutions.*
