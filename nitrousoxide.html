Relaxed Dentistry, Fast Relief

Nitrous Oxide is a highly effective and long-trusted supplement to treatment, especially if you are feeling anxious about your dental visit. It takes effect almost immediately and is easily customized for your comfort.

You will breathe a mixture of oxygen and nitrous oxide through a small nasal mask. This approach is completely safe: you remain fully awake , able to breathe on your own, and in control of all bodily functions throughout the procedure.

  • Rapid Onset: It works quickly, providing immediate relief.
  • Adjustable Depth: The level of sedation can be safely altered at any time.
  • No "Hangover": There is no lingering after-effect, allowing you to drive home safely.
  • Cardiac & Pulmonary Safety: Safe to use with no side effects on your heart and lungs.
  • Minimizes Gagging: Very effective at reducing the gag reflex.

üö´ Reasons to Avoid Nitrous Oxide

While generally very safe, you should not utilize nitrous oxide if you have been diagnosed with Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease (COPD) .

Other conditions where caution is advised include:

  • Emphysema
  • Exotic chest problems or recent chest surgery
  • Multiple Sclerosis (M.S.)
  • An active cold or significant nasal breathing difficulties
