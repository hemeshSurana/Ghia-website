About Us

Who we are?

Dr Maher Kemmoona is an European Federation of Periodontology (EFP) certified Periodontist. He has undergone a 3-year full time postgraduate training program at Trinity College Dublin qualifying in 2007.

He spent his earlier part of life living in Germany and qualified as a dentist there in 1999. After working in general practice for two years he went on to undertake a year program in Periodontology and Prosthetic rehabilitation of periodontal patients in Germany prior to returning to Ireland to set up a specialist dental practice here.

At our practice Dr Kemmoona deals with simple non-surgical periodontal treatments as well as the complex interdisciplinary cases that often require the assistance of other dental specialists. He will inform you when this is indicated. Often, he will work with the assistance of your own general dental practitioner to assist you with your specific needs resulting in patient specific treatment plans.

Dr Kemmoona continues to engage with dental and medical colleagues internationally to further his knowledge of dental implants and periodontal therapy to offer cutting edge therapy to his patients.
