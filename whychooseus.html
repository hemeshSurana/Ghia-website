WHY CHOOSE OUR PRACTICE?

We know that you have options when it comes to picking a dentist. Personalized, gentle, comprehensive patient care in a comfortable setting is our goal.

Our practice is committed to these core values:

Cleanliness, Care, and Comfort

We are committed to providing appropriate dental care to you and your family. We will always provide honest answers to your questions and give you options to choose from as you make informed decisions about your dental care. From our comfortable front office to the exam room, our practice is made up of friendly people who take your oral health very seriously. Using state-of-the-art equipment designed with safety and efficiency in mind, we are able to offer top-notch care. As you walk through our office, you will notice that everything is clean, comfortable, and well maintained. Your safety and comfort are our priority.

Information and Honesty

We know that the cost of care and your dental plan can be confusing. We will give you up-front information on your treatment ahead of time to avoid surprises. If you provide your insurance information, we will research your benefits to educate you about your options. We will gladly file your insurance claims for you and help you navigate your dental insurance and alternative payment options.

Efficiency and Availability

We know that your time is valuable to you. We always strive to minimize waiting, provide efficient treatment, and finish your appointment on time. We provide dental appointment reminders and a quick turnaround time on calls and appointment requests. We will answer any questions or concerns in the office, over the phone, or via email.

Comprehensive Dental Care in One Location

At Dr. Ghia, DDS, MAGD, we are qualified to handle all aspects of your care, so you are always surrounded by people that you trust. We see patients from 6 months old to over 100 years of age. If we need to refer out, we promise to send you to colleagues who will take good care of you.

Relax!

We want you to be comfortable and involved in deciding what treatment is appropriate for you. We want you to feel in control of your oral health. We also offer Nitrous Oxide as a supplement to aid in your relaxation, if that is appropriate.

Go the Extra Smile!

You arent just a patient; we value your relationship. We take an interest in you and your oral health needs. We think youll enjoy forming friendships with our staff during your visit!

Give us call today at (940) 692-8411! You'll be happy you choose us for your dental needs !
