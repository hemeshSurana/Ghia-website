✨ SOFT DENTURE LINERS

A soft liner is a layer of soft, pliable material that is fitted between the surface of a denture and your oral tissues. It acts as a **shock absorber** between the hard acrylic base of your denture and your gums. Soft liners can be used when creating new dentures or **retrofitted** into existing dentures for enhanced comfort.

Who Benefits from Permanent Soft Liners?

Permanent soft liners provide lasting comfort and relief for individuals with specific challenges:

  • Patients with **Receded and flattened gum tissues** that do not respond well to the stress of hard-base dentures.
  • Individuals experiencing **chronically sore gum tissues**.
  • Gums with **sharp bony areas** (knife-edge ridges), where the soft material offers necessary cushioning.

Key Advantages of Soft Liners

  • **Enhanced Comfort:** Dentures become significantly more comfortable to use.
  • **High Acceptance Rate:** Virtually all patients respond well to soft liner materials.
  • **Improved Function:** Facilitates easier and more stable chewing.
  • **Proven Reliability:** A well-established technology with over 20 years of innovation.
