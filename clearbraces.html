Clear Aligners: The Invisible Orthodontic Choice

Clear Aligners (often referred to as clear braces or Invisalign) offer the invisible way to straighten your teeth without the hassle of metal wires or fixed brackets.

These removable aligners are crafted using Dr. Ghia's expert prescription combined with advanced 3-D computer imaging technology.

[Image of clear dental aligners on teeth]

The Treatment Process

Sequential Alignment

You wear each set of aligners for approximately 2 weeks . As you replace each aligner with the next in the series, your teeth gradually move into the final prescribed position.

*Aligners must be removed only for eating, drinking, brushing, and flossing.*

Progress Monitoring

You'll visit Dr. Ghia about once every 6 weeks to ensure your treatment is progressing exactly as planned. Total treatment time is customized based on your individual case complexity.
